🛡️ Privacy Policy for Anti-Doom Scroller

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Anti-Doom Scroller ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") is developed and published by Auzuru. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information and data on your device.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not collect or share personal information with us. All usage data is stored locally on your device only.

The App may access limited data locally for functionality:

  • App usage time: Measured via Android's Usage Access permission to calculate doomscrolling time on selected apps.
  • Ad interactions: Google AdMob may collect anonymized usage data and device identifiers to display ads. This data is handled entirely by Google and is subject to their privacy policy.

We do not collect names, emails, or personal identifiers, or send data to our own servers. Our advertising partner, Google AdMob, may track users across apps or websites for ad personalization purposes — see their privacy policy for details.

3. Data Storage and Retention

All collected data (such as daily and total doomscrolling time) is stored locally on your device only. Daily data resets automatically at a user-configured time each day. All app data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app. No remote storage or cloud synchronization is used.

4. Data Deletion

Since no personal data is stored remotely, you can delete all app data by clearing the app's storage in Android settings or by uninstalling the app.

5. Permissions Used

  • Usage Access Permission: To detect which app is currently in use and measure screen time on selected apps. Used exclusively for app functionality.
  • Overlay Permission (Display over other apps): To show a break reminder screen when needed.
  • Notifications Permission: To display the background monitoring notification required by Android for foreground services.
  • Modify Audio Settings: To temporarily mute audio during break reminders and restore it afterward.
  • Ignore Battery Optimizations: To request that Android keeps the monitoring service running reliably in the background.
  • Internet / Network State: Used by Google AdMob to serve and measure ads.

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit personal data by us.

6. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may collect and use the Android Advertising ID to deliver and measure ads, limit ad frequency, and detect fraud. This identifier is not linked to any personally identifiable information and can be reset or disabled in your device settings.

We use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain your consent for personalized advertising in regions where required (such as the EEA and UK). You can update your consent choice at any time through your device settings.

For details, visit: Google's Privacy Policy

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13 years of age.

8. Data Security

Since all app data is stored locally on your device, it never leaves your device through us. No external transmission means no third-party access to your personal data by us. Google AdMob's data handling is governed by Google's own security practices.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
